# 📕 **5. CSC 441 – Senior Design I**

*File: `CSC_441_Senior_Design_I.md`*

## Course Description

CSC 441 is the first course in the Senior Design sequence. Students work in teams to propose, design, and plan a substantial software or hardware system. The course emphasizes communication, project planning, requirements engineering, and ethical decision-making.

## Learning Outcomes

Students will be able to:

* Formulate project requirements and specifications
* Conduct feasibility studies and risk assessments
* Develop a project proposal and design document
* Work effectively in teams
* Apply ethical analysis and communication skills

## Major Topics

* Software engineering lifecycle
* Requirements analysis
* System architecture and design patterns
* Project management & Agile methods
* Professional writing & technical communication
* Ethics in software engineering

## Assessments

* Requirements document
* Design document
* Proposal presentation
* Weekly team reports
* Professionalism evaluation

## Expected Weekly Workload

8–12 hours per week (team meetings, writing, planning)

## Prerequisites

Senior standing and completion/co-enrollment of major upper-division CS requirements.
