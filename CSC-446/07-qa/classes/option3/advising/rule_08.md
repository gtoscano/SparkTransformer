## **8. Internship / Practical Experience Recommendation**

Although not required, students are **strongly encouraged** to complete an internship or research experience before graduation.
Advisors should recommend taking:

* *CSC 491/492 Independent Study* or
* an internship for credit
  during junior or senior year if the student seeks practical experience.

---

## **9. Senior Design Eligibility Rule**

To enroll in Senior Design (e.g., CSC 441 / 442), students must:

* Have completed all 200-level CS courses,
* Be enrolled in or have completed key 300-level courses (OS, Networks, Databases depending on catalog),
* Be in good academic standing.
  Advisors must verify progress BEFORE approving Senior Design registration.

---

## **10. Advising Guideline for Students Falling Behind**

If a student fails or withdraws from a core CS course, the following actions are recommended:

1. Do not take more than 12–14 credits next semester.
2. Avoid enrolling in upper-division CS courses until the foundational course is retaken.
3. Schedule monthly check-ins with the advisor.
4. Consider tutoring, office hours, and supplemental instruction support.
